How to Use vizio.com/setup to Activate Your VIZIO TV

If you or a loved one have difficulty with small text or mobility, this page is designed to help. Use screen readers, keyboard arrows, or zoom features easily.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Visit vizio.com/setup on your browser
  2. Log in with your VIZIO account (or create one)
  3. On your TV: Go to Home → Extras → VIZIO Account
  4. Enter the 6-digit code shown on your TV
  5. Your TV is now linked!

Why It’s Important to Link Your TV

  • Access exclusive apps and offers
  • Get firmware updates easily
  • Control settings via mobile

Having Issues?

If the code doesn’t work or times out, try restarting the TV and rechecking Extras → VIZIO Account. You can also contact Vizio support.
